How to Use SkyWatch

Navigating through SkyWatch to access accurate weather forecasts for your desired city is a breeze. With a user-friendly interface and simple steps, you'll be equipped with essential weather information in no time. Here's a step-by-step guide on how to effectively use SkyWatch:

Step 1: Access the Website

Open your preferred web browser and go to the SkyWatch website. The homepage welcomes you with an inviting layout and a prominently displayed search box.

Step 2: City Search

Locate the Search Box: In the center of the homepage, you'll find the search box where you can input the city name for which you want to retrieve weather information.

Enter the City Name: Type the name of the city you're interested in knowing the weather for. Make sure to spell the city name correctly to ensure accurate results.

Initiate the Search: Press the "Enter" key on your keyboard after typing the city name, or simply click the magnifying glass icon located next to the search box.

Step 3: Weather Information

Upon submitting the city name, SkyWatch processes your request and promptly displays the weather information for the city you searched. You'll be presented with details such as temperature, humidity, wind speed, wind direction, and more.

Troubleshooting:

Invalid City Name: If you see the message "Error: Invalid name, please check the city name correctly," it indicates that there might be a spelling mistake in the city name you entered. Double-check the spelling and ensure it matches the actual name of the city.

Empty Search Box: If you encounter the message "Search Box is Empty" after clicking the search button or pressing enter, it's a reminder that you attempted to search without inputting any city name. Make sure you've typed the city name before initiating the search.

Exploring Random Cities:

For a touch of spontaneity, SkyWatch provides a selection of five random cities below the weather results. These cities are generated at random and are beyond your control. If you're interested in exploring weather forecasts for different cities, simply reload the page, and a new set of random cities will appear.

By following these straightforward steps and tips, you'll quickly become proficient at using SkyWatch to access reliable weather forecasts. Whether you're planning a trip, preparing for an outdoor event, or simply curious about the weather in your area, SkyWatch has you covered with accurate and up-to-date information.
